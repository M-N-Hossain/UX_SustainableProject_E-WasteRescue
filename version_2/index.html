Discover More

Together, we can transform our relationship with electronic devices and create a more sustainable future.

Responsible Recycling

Proper e-waste recycling recovers valuable resources that can be reused in manufacturing new devices.

Our network of certified recyclers ensures that 95% of materials are recovered by weight, reducing mining impacts drastically.

Repair & Refurbishment

Extending the lifespan of electronics is often the most environmentally friendly option.

Our repair guides and refurbishment centers help keep valuable devices working longer and out of landfills.

Fighting Planned Obsolescence

Challenge the culture of constant upgrades by supporting manufacturers who design for longevity.

We advocate for right-to-repair policies to give you more control over devices you own.
